## 3. Definitions

This Section defines the capitalised terms used throughout the Agreement.

**"Affiliate"** means any entity that controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with a Party, where "control" means ownership of more than fifty percent (50%) of the voting securities or the power to direct management.

**"Documentation"** means Vendor's then-current technical and end-user documentation for the Service made available at `{{Documentation URL}}` or in-product.

**"Personal Data"** has the meaning given in Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR"), namely any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

**"Service"** means the Vendor cloud-based software-as-a-service offering identified in the applicable Order Form, including any Documentation, support and updates provided as part of that offering.

**"Service Generated Data"** means logs, metadata, telemetry, configuration data and usage statistics produced by Vendor's operation of the Service that are not Customer Data, used by Vendor to operate, secure, support and improve the Service.

**"Vendor Technology"** means the Service, Documentation, the underlying software, models, algorithms, infrastructure, know-how, and any modifications, derivatives or improvements of any of the foregoing, in each case excluding Customer Data.
